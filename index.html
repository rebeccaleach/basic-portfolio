About Me

I’m Rebecca, an editor and web development student. Languages are my thing, whether they’re spoken and written, visual, or code. I do developmental and line edits for young adult manuscripts and copyediting and proofreading for just about anything. You can find out more about my book editing services at Scarlet Leaf Editing. Currently, I’m a student at the University of Texas at Austin’s coding bootcamp program, scheduled to graduate in January 2017. I’ll be a full-stack developer, focusing on JavaScript and PHP development, and I’m incredibly excited about it!
